About Us

✨ Crafting Elegance with Every Stitch

Charvi's Maggam Hub specializes in computerized machine embroidery and traditional maggam work designs. We create beautiful and detailed embroidery for blouses, sarees, lehengas, and designer outfits, combining modern technology with traditional craftsmanship.

Our goal is to provide high-quality embroidery with perfect finishing and creative designs that enhance the beauty of every outfit. Whether it is bridal wear, festive clothing, or custom embroidery, we carefully design each piece to match our customers’ style and expectations.

At Charvi's Maggam Hub, we focus on precision, creativity, and customer satisfaction, ensuring every design reflects elegance and uniqueness.

Located in Jeypore, Koraput, we proudly serve customers looking for professional embroidery work with both traditional and modern touch.
